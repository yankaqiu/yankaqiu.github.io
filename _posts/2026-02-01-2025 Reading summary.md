This year, most of my reading happened on screens.

Somehow, e-books quietly took over my life — easier to carry, easier to start, harder to remember. I missed the physical feeling of books a little, though. At the end of the year, I bought several beautiful paper books while traveling in London, and I hope next year will bring me back to slower, more tangible reading moments.

Here’s what stayed with me.

Fiction

The Book of Goose
好看。
Quiet but unsettling. The kind of story that feels gentle on the surface but leaves a strange echo afterwards.

Anxious People — Fredrik Backman
好看。读完直接入了 Backman 的好几本。
Warm, human, and unexpectedly comforting. It feels like someone understands modern awkwardness and loneliness very precisely.

Yellowface — R.F. Kuang
好看，但结尾一般。
Sharp and entertaining satire about publishing, identity, and ambition. I enjoyed the ride more than the destination.

百年孤独 (One Hundred Years of Solitude)
终于读完。
像进入一个潮湿、缓慢、不断循环的梦。读的时候经常迷路，但也正是它的魅力。

长安的荔枝
飞机上的完美消遣读物。
轻快、流畅，非常适合旅途中读。

消失的13级台阶
还可以。
没有想象中那么多反转，某些情节让我有点不舒服，但也许这正是作者想制造的道德压力。

羊道三部曲
带来一种奇怪的宁静。
人与自然、迁徙与生活节奏，让人慢下来。

东京八平米
浴室最佳读物。
学会享受自己的一点地盘，与窘迫的当下和解。

⸻

Non-fiction

Everything Is Predictable: How Bayesian Statistics Explain Our World
再次回到 Bayesian thinking。世界似乎比想象中更可预测，也更不确定。
Probably influenced by my studies more than I expected.
Once you start seeing the world probabilistically, it’s hard to go back.

The World I See — Albert Einstein
Short reflections, surprisingly personal.
Not only about physics, but about humanity.

Invisible Cities — Italo Calvino
不像传统 non-fiction，更像哲学式想象。
每一座城市都是一种观看世界的方式。

我不相信神话
一些观念上的松动时刻。
读完会开始重新审视很多“理所当然”。

控糖革命
让我重新思考饮食习惯。
知识含量 > 实际执行力。

How Bad Do You Want It?
关于极限运动与心理韧性。
More mental than physical.

The Culture Map — Erin Meyer
在国际环境生活的人应该都会有共鸣。
解释了很多“为什么沟通会怪怪的瞬间”。

Reality Is Broken
Games are not just entertainment — they are systems of motivation.
读完后对 gamification 有新的理解。

Outlive: The Science and Art of Longevity — Peter Attia
Health suddenly feels like a long-term engineering project. Shared it with my parents already.
